Key Findings

The study compared two object detection algorithms, Faster R-CNN and YOLOv8, for helmet detection in construction sites.

  • Faster R-CNN: High accuracy (~25.34% mAP@0.5) but slow (142ms per image).
  • YOLOv8: Higher accuracy (~63.4% mAP@0.5) and significantly faster (~10ms per image).
  • Conclusion: YOLOv8 is more suitable for real-time safety monitoring.
